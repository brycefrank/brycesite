Personal Profile

PhD candidate in forest biometrics with a specific affinity for remote sensing applications in large scale forest inventory systems, novel statistical methodology and software development. My particular focus is on the rigorous assessment of uncertainty in forest inventories at multiple spatial scales through the use of small area estimation.
